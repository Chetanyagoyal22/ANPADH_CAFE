Our Story

From a Dream to Reality

Anpadh Cafe was founded in 2015 by Arun Saldana in the vibrant city of Dehradun, Uttarakhand. What started as a small food stall has now grown into a beloved local eatery known for its authentic North Indian cuisine.

The name "Anpadh" (meaning 'unlettered' in Hindi) reflects our philosophy that you don't need fancy culinary degrees to create amazing food - just passion, tradition, and love for cooking. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, carrying the authentic tastes of North Indian homes.

Located near Arihant Hostels in Pondha, we started with the mission to provide college students and working professionals with affordable, homestyle meals that remind them of their mother's cooking.

Our Mission

At Anpadh Cafe, our mission is simple yet meaningful:

  • Authentic Taste

    Promote affordable, authentic Indian meals that preserve traditional flavors and cooking techniques.

  • Homely Experience

    Create a warm, welcoming environment that makes every guest feel at home.

  • Quality & Generosity

    Maintain high standards of hygiene and food quality while serving generous portions.

  • Celebrate Tradition

    Honor and promote local culinary traditions through our menu and cooking practices.

Meet Our Founder

Arun Saldana

Founder & Head Chef

With a passion for authentic North Indian cuisine, Arun Saldana established Anpadh Cafe in 2015. Growing up in a family where food was an expression of love, Arun learned the art of cooking from his mother and grandmother.

Before opening Anpadh Cafe, Arun worked in various kitchens across North India, honing his skills and collecting recipes. His dream was to create a space where people could enjoy homestyle meals at affordable prices.

"I believe that good food doesn't have to be complicated or expensive. The best meals are those cooked with simple ingredients and a lot of heart," says Arun.

Food is not just about filling stomachs, it's about creating memories and bringing people together.
